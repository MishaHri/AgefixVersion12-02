Pre-Treatment Advice:


  • Avoid using topical products that contain tretinoin, retinol, retinoids, glycolic acid, or any "anti-aging" products (1-2 weeks before and after )
  • Refrain from consuming alcohol, coffee or using blood thinners such as Ibuprofen/Ginkgo Biloba/St John’s Wort for 24hours prior to the treatment.
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning treatments for a week prior.
  • Ensure the skin is clean and free of any makeup or lotions on the day of treatment.

Post-Treatment Care:


  • Use gentle, non-irritating skin care products.
  • Avoid direct sun exposure and apply sunscreen regularly.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid strenuous exercise for a couple of days.
  • Do not use makeup for at least 24 hours after the treatment.

Results:

  • Visible improvements in skin texture and appearance can be seen after a few weeks.
  • For best results, a series of treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart is typically recommended.
  • The skin continues to improve over several months as collagen production increases.

About the treatment:

Experience the Transformation: Diminish Your Stretch Marks

Stretch marks, known as striae, are a common concern, affecting many individuals due to growth spurts, pregnancy, weight fluctuations, or certain medications. Contrary to the belief that they are irreversible, Age Fix Clinic offers a promising solution with our Dermapen Treatments™, designed to significantly lessen the visibility of these marks and rejuvenate your skin.

Initially appearing as red, blue, or even purple lines, stretch marks gradually transition into white, silver, or brown. Dermapen Treatments at Age Fix Clinic harness the power of advanced microneedling technology to stimulate collagen production and remodel the stretch mark tissue, effectively diminishing their appearance. This method has been recognized by both practitioners and patients as more effective than traditional stretch mark therapies like lasers or topical creams.

Clinical Procedure for Stretch Mark Reduction At Age Fix Clinic, we specialize in using Dermapen for reducing both old and new stretch marks. The state-of-the-art Dermapen 4™, with its specific scar setting, penetrates up to 3mm deep, creating 1,920 micro-channels per second. This process breaks down scar tissue and fosters new collagen, resulting in smoother, firmer, and revitalized skin.

The rapid and precise movement of the Dermapen device ensures that even larger areas, such as the abdomen or thighs, receive quick, effective treatment with minimal discomfort.

Customized Treatment Protocols Each client's journey begins with a thorough consultation at Age Fix Clinic to determine the specific needs and schedule for Dermapen treatments. We use the specially crafted Dp Dermaceuticals™ skincare range as part of the Meso-Glide™ technique, introducing restorative ingredients directly into the skin to enhance the fading of stretch marks.

Enhancing Your Results For optimal results, we recommend using the Dermapen HOME™ for safe, at-home microneedling between professional sessions at our clinic. Incorporating Dp Dermaceuticals into your skincare regimen before, during, and after your treatments not only accelerates the process but also ensures lasting and more noticeable results.
