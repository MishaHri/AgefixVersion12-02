Pre-Treatment Advice:


  • Avoid blood thinners: Prevent bruising and bleeding risks before procedure.
  • Stay hydrated: Enhance skin condition for better treatment outcomes.
  • Limit alcohol and caffeine: Reduce dehydration and potential complications.
  • Protect from sun: Prevent sun damage, crucial for optimal skin health.

Post-Treatment Care:


  • Ice for swelling: Reduce post-procedure swelling with cold compress application.
  • Gentle cleansing: Cleanse treated area gently to avoid irritation or infection.
  • Sun protection: Shield skin from sun exposure to prevent pigmentation changes.
  • Follow-up appointment: Attend scheduled follow-up for assessment and further guidance.

Maximise & Maintain Results:

  • Hydrate skin: Drink water to maintain skin elasticity and hydration levels
  • Protect from sun: Prevent sun damage to preserve filler longevity and results:.
  • Follow skincare regimen: Use recommended products for enhanced skin health and longevity.
  • Healthy lifestyle: Maintain balanced diet and skincare routine for lasting results:.

CONTRAINDICATION

  • Mild allergies: Discuss any allergies with your practitioner for safe treatment
  • Skin sensitivities: Inform your practitioner about skin conditions for tailored care.
  • Blood conditions: Some conditions may require closer monitoring but are manageable.
  • Immune health: Ensure your immune system is strong for optimal healing
  • Prior experiences: Share previous experiences for improved treatment planning.
  • Healing process: Wait until the skin has fully recovered from previous treatments.
