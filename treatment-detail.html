    Masseter reduction, often achieved through the targeted use of botulinum toxin (Botox) injections, is a non-surgical procedure designed to slim the jawline and reduce the size of the masseter muscles. These muscles, located at the sides of the jaw, can become enlarged due to genetics, teeth grinding (bruxism), or excessive chewing, leading to a more square or masculine jawline.
