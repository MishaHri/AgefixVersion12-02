TREATMENT RESTRICTIONS


Age Limitation:

Individuals under 18 are not eligible for treatment (valid ID required). Skin Medication: Treatment is not advisable for those on certain skin medications.


Cold Sores and Oral Herpes:

Individuals with active cold sores or oral herpes are not suitable for treatment.


Medical Conditions:

Those undergoing chemotherapy, experiencing epilepsy, or pregnancy, among other various medical conditions, are advised against treatment.


Allergies and Skin Conditions:

Individuals with allergies, particularly to anaesthetics like Lidocaine, and certain skin conditions are not candidates for treatment.


Recent Procedures:

We do not recommend treatment for those who have recently undergone laser or chemical peeling.


Glaucoma and Blood-Thinning Medications:

Individuals with glaucoma or those taking blood-thinning medication should refrain from treatment.


Health Concerns:

Diabetics, individuals with high blood pressure, or mitral valve disorders are advised against undergoing treatment.


Skin Conditions at Treatment Site:

Those with eczema, psoriasis, rashes, blisters, healing disorders, uncontrolled hypertension, or mitral valve disorders at the treatment site are not suitable candidates.


Epilepsy:

Individuals who have had a seizure in the past two years are advised against treatment.


Additional Health Conditions:

Treatment is not recommended for individuals who are pregnant, breastfeeding, have cancer, autoimmune diseases, active skin infections, chronic disease exacerbations, hemophilia, or skin inflammation in the treatment area.


Keloid Scars:

Individuals with a tendency to form keloid scars should not undergo treatment.
