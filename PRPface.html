Pre-Treatment Advice:


  • Avoid anti-inflammatory medications for a few days prior.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy meal before the procedure.
  • Inform your practitioner of any medications or supplements you are taking.

Post-Treatment Care:


  • Avoid touching or washing the treated area for a few hours.
  • Refrain from strenuous activities for at least 24 hours.
  • Apply a cold compress if swelling occurs.
  • Follow any specific aftercare instructions given by your practitioner.

Results:

  • Results vary but generally begin to show within a few weeks of treatment.
  • Multiple sessions may be needed for optimal results, depending on the treatment area and goals.
  • The effects can last for several months to a year, with touch-ups as needed

Contraindications:

    The treatment it is not recommended for who presents one of the following condition:

  • Scalp infections or inflammatory skin conditions.
  • Alopecia areata (an autoimmune condition causing hair loss, where PRP may not be effective).
  • Blood clotting disorders.
  • Recent steroid injections to the scalp.

About the treatment:

What is PRP Therapy?
PRP Therapy harnesses the power of your own blood to rejuvenate and heal your skin. It involves extracting a small sample of your blood, separating the platelets to create a concentrated plasma, and then re-injecting this into your skin. This plasma is rich in growth factors that stimulate natural healing and rejuvenation. How Does PRP Therapy Work for Skin Rejuvenation? PRP Therapy stimulates collagen production and accelerates the skin’s natural healing process. It's effective in reducing wrinkles, fine lines, and improving overall skin texture and tone. Who Benefits from PRP Therapy? Ideal for individuals seeking a natural approach to skin rejuvenation, PRP Therapy is suitable for anyone looking to reduce signs of aging, improve skin texture, or heal scars and marks. Why Choose PRP Therapy Over Other Treatments? PRP Therapy is a natural, minimally invasive option that uses your body’s own healing mechanisms. It's perfect for those who prefer an organic approach to skin care without the use of synthetic materials or chemicals. What Results Can I Expect? Following PRP Therapy, you can expect to see a gradual improvement in skin texture and tone. The skin becomes firmer, more radiant, and youthful. Results vary among individuals but generally develop over a few weeks to months. How Many Treatments Are Required? The number of PRP sessions varies based on individual goals and skin conditions. Typically, a series of 3-4 treatments spaced about a month apart is recommended for optimal results. What is the Downtime and Recovery Like? PRP Therapy involves minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness or swelling post-treatment, but this usually subsides quickly, allowing you to return to your normal activities. Is PRP Therapy Safe? Yes, PRP Therapy is safe and natural. As it uses your own blood, the risk of allergic reactions or infections is significantly reduced. How Soon Can I Start PRP Therapy? You can begin your PRP Therapy journey after a detailed consultation with our specialists to determine your suitability for the treatment.
