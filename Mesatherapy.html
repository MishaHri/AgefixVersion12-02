Pre-Treatment Advice:


  • Avoid using topical products that contain tretinoin, retinol, retinoids, glycolic acid, or any "anti-aging" products (1-2 weeks before and after)
  • Refrain from consuming alcohol, coffee or using blood thinners such as Ibuprofen/Ginkgo Biloba/St John’s Wort for 24hours prior to the treatment.
  • Stay hydrated and have a healthy meal before the session.
  • Ensure the treatment area is clean and free of makeup.

Post-Treatment Care


  • Avoid touching the treated area for a few hours.
  • Refrain from high-intensity exercise for 24-48 hours.
  • Avoid exposing the treated area to extreme heat or cold for a few days.
  • Exercise: Hold off on intense physical workouts for up to 48 hours post-treatment.

Results

  • The rejuvenating effects can be observed after a few sessions.
  • For optimal results, a series of treatments may be recommended.
  • Maintenance sessions can be scheduled as per individual needs to sustain the benefits.

Teatment Sumary

    What is Mesotherapy?
    Mesotherapy is a minimally invasive procedure that involves the injection of a custom blend of vitamins, enzymes, hormones, and plant extracts to rejuvenate and tighten skin, as well as remove excess fat. How Does Mesotherapy Work?
    This technique uses very fine needles to deliver a series of injections into the middle layer (mesoderm) of the skin. The infusion of ingredients is tailored to your specific skin needs, addressing issues like dullness, aging, and hydration. Who Benefits from Mesotherapy?
    Mesotherapy is ideal for anyone looking to improve the appearance and quality of their skin. It's particularly beneficial for those dealing with skin aging, pigmentation, fine lines, and mild sagging. Why Choose Mesotherapy Over Other Skin Treatments? Mesotherapy offers a customized solution that directly targets your skin concerns at their source. It's less invasive than surgical procedures and can be tailored to suit all skin types and concerns. What Results Can I Expect?
    Mesotherapy improves skin tone, reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, and gives a natural, rejuvenated look. The results are gradual and cumulative, with optimal benefits seen after a series of treatments. How Many Treatments Are Required?
    The number of sessions varies depending on your individual skin condition and goals. Typically, a series of 4-6 treatments are recommended for best results, spaced out over several weeks. Is There Downtime with Mesotherapy?
    Mesotherapy has minimal downtime. You may experience some redness, bruising, or swelling, which typically resolves quickly. Is Mesotherapy Safe?
    Yes, when performed by qualified professionals, Mesotherapy is safe. The treatment uses substances that are well-tolerated by the skin, minimizing the risk of allergic reactions. How Soon Can I Start My Treatment?
