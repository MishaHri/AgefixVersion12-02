Pre-Treatment:


  • Avoid alcohol, caffeine, and blood thinners like Ibuprofen, Ginkgo Biloba, and St John’s Wort 24 hours before your treatment.
  • Stay out of the sun and abstain from tanning treatments for one week leading up to the procedure
  • Refrain from using products with acids for 48 hours prior to the treatment.
  • Make sure your skin is clean and free from any hair products on the day of your treatment

Post-Treatment:


  • Keep away from direct sunlight and ensure regular sunscreen application.
  • Stay well-hydrated and limit strenuous activities for a few days.
  • Refrain from using hair products or washing your hair for 24 hours following your treatment.
  • Avoid using saunas, steam rooms, and swimming pools for 48 hours post-treatment.
  • Postpone any intense physical exercise, as well as exposure to sun and heat, for at least 48 hours after your treatment

Results:

  • Stimulation of Hair Growth: Dermapen treatments on the scalp can stimulate natural hair growth by increasing blood flow and nutrient supply to the hair follicles.
  • Enhanced Absorption of Topical Products: The micro-injuries created by the Dermapen can improve the absorption of topical treatments applied to the scalp, making them more effective.
  • Increased Collagen and Elastin Production: Similar to its effects on the skin, microneedling can boost collagen and elastin production in the scalp, which may contribute to healthier and stronger hair.
  • Thicker Hair: Some individuals may notice an increase in the thickness of their hair, as the treatment can rejuvenate and strengthen hair follicles.
  • Reduction in Hair Loss: Regular microneedling sessions can help in reducing the rate of hair loss by strengthening the hair roots.
  • Improved Scalp Health: The treatment can also lead to an overall improvement in scalp health, reducing issues like dryness or flakiness.
  • Gradual Results: The effects of microneedling with Dermapen are typically gradual. Consistent treatment sessions are often necessary to see significant improvements.
  • Minimal Downtime: This non-invasive procedure usually requires minimal to no downtime, making it a convenient option for those with busy schedules.
  • Safe for Various Hair Types: Microneedling is generally safe for all hair types and can be adjusted in intensity to suit individual needs and scalp sensitivities.
  • Variable Outcomes: As with any cosmetic procedure, results can vary based on individual factors like age, genetics, the extent of hair thinning, and overall scalp health.
