Pre-Treatment:


  • Avoid using topical products that contain tretinoin, retinol, retinoids, glycolic acid, or any "anti-aging" products.
  • Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications (like aspirin or ibuprofen) for at least 24 hours before the treatment to reduce the risk of bruising.
  • Stay hydrated, but avoid excessive liquids right before the session to reduce discomfort.
  • Ensure the treatment area is clean and free of makeup, creams, and lotions.
  • Consult with your practitioner regarding any supplements or medications you are taking, as some may need to be paused.

Post-Treatment:


  • Avoid using topical products that contain tretinoin, retinol, retinoids, glycolic acid, or any "anti-aging" products.
  • Avoid touching or applying pressure to the treated area for at least 6 hours post-treatment to prevent infection and allow the product to settle.
  • Refrain from intense physical activity, sauna, and steam rooms for a couple of days to minimize swelling and bruising.
  • Avoid extreme temperatures (very cold or hot) on the treated area for a few days.
  • Postpone any other facial treatments for at least a week after Profhilo®treatment.
  • Follow any specific aftercare instructions provided by your practitioner.

Results:

  • Initial improvements in skin hydration and texture can be noticed within a few days.
  • Optimal results:, including firmer, more radiant skin with reduced fine lines and wrinkles, are typically observed about a month after treatment.
  • The effects of Profhilo® generally last around six months, after which a maintenance treatment may be recommended to sustain results:.
