About the treatment:

What is PRP Therapy for the Scalp?

PRP Therapy for the scalp is an innovative treatment that uses your own blood's platelet-rich plasma to stimulate hair growth and improve scalp health. This procedure involves drawing a small amount of your blood, processing it to concentrate the platelets, and reinjecting it into your scalp.

How it Works: A small amount of blood is drawn from the patient and then spun in a centrifuge to separate the plasma and concentrate the platelets. This platelet-rich plasma is then injected back into the treatment area, releasing growth factors that stimulate natural healing processes, collagen production, and cell growth.

Is it Safe? Yes, PRP is generally safe as it uses the patient's own blood, minimizing the risk of allergic reactions or infections. However, like any procedure involving injections, there is a risk of bruising, swelling, or infection.

How Does PRP Therapy Promote Hair Growth?
The concentrated platelets in PRP are rich in growth factors that can stimulate dormant hair follicles, promote new hair growth, and improve the health and thickness of existing hair.

Who Benefits from PRP Scalp Therapy?
This treatment is ideal for those experiencing hair thinning, hair loss, or scalp health issues. It's suitable for both men and women seeking a natural approach to hair restoration.

PRP Therapy for the scalp involves minimal downtime, allowing you to resume normal activities shortly after the treatment. Some clients may experience mild redness or tenderness at the injection sites.
