Pre-Treatment:


  • Refrain from taking blood-thinning medications and supplements to reduce the risk of bruising.
  • Stay well-hydrated and avoid alcohol consumption before the treatment.
  • Inform your practitioner of any allergies or existing medical conditions.

Post-Treatment:


  • Apply ice or a cold compress to minimize swelling or discomfort.
  • Temporarily avoid rigorous physical activities.
  • Adhere to any specific instructions given by your healthcare provider.

Results:

  • Immediately but the full effect becomes evident a few weeks after treatment, as the body processes and removes the dissolved fat cells.
  • Optimal results: might require multiple sessions.
  • The outcomes are typically long-lasting, particularly with a healthy lifestyle.

Contraindications:

  • Allergy to any ingredients in the injectable solution.
  • Active infection in the treatment area.
  • Blood clotting disorders.
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding.
  • Severe liver or kidney disease.
